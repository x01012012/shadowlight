Join Our Team as a Private Investigation Agent

Are you detail-oriented, observant, and passionate about uncovering the truth? Do you thrive in dynamic environments where no two days are the same? Join ShadowLight Investigations as a Private Investigation Agent and take on an exciting, rewarding career.

Why Choose This Career?

  • Work with Purpose: Help clients resolve personal, legal, corporate, and financial issues.
  • Flexible Hours: Schedule varies based on assignments and client needs.
  • Diverse Cases: Handle surveillance, background checks, fraud investigations, and missing persons cases.
  • Professional Growth: Gain experience across industries and develop transferable skills.
  • Make an Impact: Your findings can influence court decisions, business strategies, and personal lives.

Job Profile & Responsibilities

  • Conduct discreet and effective surveillance and stakeouts.
  • Gather evidence through interviews, public records, and online research.
  • Prepare detailed reports and summaries for clients or legal teams.
  • Collaborate with lawyers, insurance companies, and businesses.
  • Maintain strict confidentiality and professionalism.
  • Use technology like GPS tracking, databases, and forensic software.
  • Comply with local laws and ethical standards.

Requirements

  • Strong observational and analytical skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication.
  • Discretion, integrity, and sound judgment.
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple cases.
  • Willingness to travel and work outside normal hours when needed.
  • Prior experience in law enforcement, military, or legal fields (preferred but not mandatory).
  • Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation for fieldwork.

Incentives & Benefits

  • Competitive Pay: Performance-based salary with overtime and case bonuses.
  • Training Programs: Comprehensive training on investigative techniques, legal compliance, and safety.
  • Tools & Equipment: Access to professional-grade surveillance gear, software, and databases.
  • Career Advancement: Specialize in areas like cyber-investigations, corporate fraud, or forensic analysis.
  • Health & Wellness: Optional health benefits for full-time agents.
  • Referral Bonuses: Earn extra for referring qualified candidates.
  • Field Allowances: Reimbursement for travel, meals, and incidentals.
