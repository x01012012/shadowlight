What to Know Before Hiring Our Investigators

At ShadowLight Investigations, transparency and trust are the foundation of our client relationships. Below is essential information to ensure a smooth, effective partnership.

Key Information

  1. Legal Compliance:
    • All investigations comply with state and federal laws. We avoid illegal practices (e.g., hacking, wiretapping, trespassing).
    • Evidence is court-admissible and ethically sourced.
  2. Confidentiality Assurance:
    • Your privacy is paramount, protected by strict NDAs and encrypted communications.
    • Case details are never shared with third parties.
  3. Scope & Limitations:
    • We Can:
      • Perform background checks using public records and licensed databases.
      • Conduct surveillance in public spaces or with proper authorization.
      • Trace digital footprints legally (e.g., social media, financial trails).
    • We Cannot:
      • Access private accounts (e.g., bank, email) without legal consent.
      • Guarantee specific outcomes (e.g., proof of infidelity, locating a person).
  4. Pricing & Fees:
    • Free initial consultation to discuss your case.
    • Transparent billing: Costs vary by case complexity (hourly rates or flat fees).
    • Average hourly rate: $75–$150, depending on investigator expertise.
    • Retainer fees may apply for long-term cases.
    • No hidden charges; all costs outlined upfront with written approval for extras.
  5. Timeline Expectations:
    • Cases range from 24 hours to several weeks, depending on evidence availability, subject cooperation, or legal permissions.
    • Urgent cases may incur rush fees.
  6. Client Responsibilities:
    • Provide accurate details (e.g., full names, last known addresses, photos).
    • Disclose potential risks (e.g., subject’s violent history, legal restrictions).
    • Avoid interfering with the investigation (e.g., confronting the subject).
  7. Ethical Standards:
    • We prioritize integrity and may decline unethical or unlawful cases.
    • Child custody and infidelity cases are handled with sensitivity and objectivity.
  8. Reporting & Communication:
    • Weekly updates via your preferred method (email, phone, secure portal).
    • Final reports include photos, videos (if applicable), and detailed summaries.
    • 24/7 emergency line for urgent developments.
  9. Post-Investigation Support:
    • Consultations for legal action or next steps (e.g., sharing evidence with attorneys).
    • Secure storage of case files for 90 days (extendable upon request).

  • All provided information is truthful and legally obtained.
